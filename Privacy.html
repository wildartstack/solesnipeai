Privacy Policy

Data Privacy for Solsnipe Algorithmic Trading.

**Effective Date: January 2025
**Last Updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Solsnipe collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you access or use our services, including our AI-powered trading bot, our website at Solsnipeai.com, and any related products or services (collectively, the "Services").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about our data practices, particularly as they relate to the unique environment of decentralized technology and blockchain transactions.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information about you from various sources, including information you provide directly to us, and information we collect automatically through your use of the Services.

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: If you contact us for support or inquiries, we may collect your email address or other contact details.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically (Usage Data)

  • Wallet Addresses: We collect and store your public blockchain wallet addresses (e.g., Ethereum address, Bitcoin address) when you connect them to our Services. These addresses are public identifiers on the blockchain.
  • Blockchain Data: Because blockchain transactions are immutable and public, we may view, collect, and analyze data related to your on-chain activities (e.g., transaction IDs, token balances, smart contract interactions). This information is already public on the respective blockchain.
  • Trading and Performance Data: We collect and analyze data related to the trades executed by the AI bot on your behalf, including trade history, order status, execution prices, and profitability metrics, to track bot performance.
  • Technical Data: We automatically record information about how you access and use the Services, including your Internet Protocol (IP) address, operating system, browser type, device information, and referral URLs.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior, remember your preferences, and track the effectiveness of our site usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following business and commercial purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Services: To operate the platform, connect your wallet, facilitate interactions with smart contracts, and ensure the Services function correctly.
  • Model Training and Algorithmic Improvement: We use aggregated and anonymized trading data, market data, and usage data to train, evaluate, and improve the underlying AI/machine learning models and trading algorithms that power our bot.
  • Security and Compliance: To monitor, detect, prevent, and respond to potential fraud, unauthorized access, or illegal activities.
  • Communications: To send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages, or to respond to your inquiries.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage trends, measure performance, and improve the user experience, features, and functionality of our Services.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We are selective about how we share your information.

3.1 Public Blockchain Transparency

**Crucially, any interaction you make on the blockchain via the Services is permanently public, visible, and traceable.** We have no control over the privacy or security of this public blockchain data.

3.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with third parties that perform services on our behalf and who require access to your information to perform those services, such as:

  • Hosting Providers (for website operation).
  • Analytics Providers (Google Analytics, to understand traffic).
  • Security Services (for monitoring threats).
  • Machine Learning Infrastructure (if we use external services to host or train our AI models).

3.3 Legal Obligations

We may disclose your information if we believe disclosure is required by any applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Security and Retention

We take reasonable measures, including organizational, technical, and physical safeguards, to help protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the security of our systems 100%.

We retain the information we collect for as long as necessary to provide the Services, and for legitimate and essential business purposes, such as maintaining the performance of the Services, making data-driven business decisions, and complying with our legal obligations.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you (excluding public blockchain data, which we cannot delete).

  • Access/Correction: You may request to review or amend the non-public personal data we have stored.
  • Opt-Out of Communications: You may opt out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the instructions in those emails.
